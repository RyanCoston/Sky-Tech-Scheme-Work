About Me

My name is Ryan Coston, I am 37 years old and from Basildon in Essex. I live with my fiancee Kirsty and our son Oliver who is 2.

Hobbies & Interests

In my spare time I enjoy days out with my son, travelling whenever possible, watching football, Formula 1 & boxing and despite my Essex heritage am a mad Newcastle United fan due to loving watching them play when I was discovering football as a kid. Music is a huge passion of mine and I am in my element most when listening to something with plenty of guitars and a drumkit, please feel free to click the below Apple Music icon to explore music for yourself. Live music is also a huge passion of mine and I have been to hundreds of gigs, festivals and stadium shows over the years.

Career History

I spent 5 years in the Royal Signals between 2003 and 2008 and during that time spent 6 months guarding the Royal Palaces in London/Windsor and 6 months based in Afghanistan working predominantly in satellite communciations. Upon leaving the Army I spent around 2 years working for Virgin Media as a service engineer finding and repairing faults on customers' home tv, broadband and telephone. I started working for AVC in 2010 as a sub contractor to Sky and then in 2013 joined Sky. I have worked for Sky in various different roles for just over 12 years and am currently an MDU Engineer working predominantly on communal satellite and residential fibre optic installations and servicing.
